More about Owen

I design, code and maintain DGI web services. DGI is always adapting to the needs of its clients with innovation and imagination. Its three guidebooks set the standard for advertising of products and services, and the Ibiza Formentera APP offers a free digital portal for all of this information. In addition, DGI publishes Ibiza Optimista – the Good News newspaper, and IbiCasa – the island’s most diverse magazine. All of our publications are available in multiple languages so that they reach both the national and international communities.
